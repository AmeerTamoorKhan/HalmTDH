About

HalmTDH is a smart and easy-to-use image analysis tool designed to help researchers, breeders, and agronomists keep track of crop development—right from the field. 📸✨

Whether you're counting sepals or identifying heads on crops like wheat, barley, rye, or triticale, HalmTDH is here to make your work faster and more accurate. 🌱🌾

Simply choose your crop, upload an image, and let the system do the rest! The tool automatically processes your image in real time—streaming annotated patches right to your screen and updating the total count as it goes. 🖼️📊

We’re combining computer vision with plant science to bring field phenotyping into the future—one image at a time. 💡🌍

HalmTDH can also analyze stubble: it computes the number of stubble that stand erect and estimates the diameter and thickness of each stubble ring with precision. 🌾📏
